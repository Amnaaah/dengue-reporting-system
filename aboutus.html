About Us

Welcome to DenGuard! We are a dedicated team of students passionate about combining technology to address real-world problems. Our platform, DenGuard, is designed to aid communities in reporting dengue breeding sites and raising awareness about dengue prevention.

Our Objectives

  • Provide a simple way for users to report dengue breeding sites.
  • Offer valuable data for authorities to act on.
  • Educate the public about symptoms, preventions and info on dengue.
